The Cheetah Page

Cheetah "Facts"

Cheetahs are fast cats with a taste for the good life. They have grace and style but suffer from low genetic diversity. This problem resulted from a serious population bottleneck that ocurred somewhere on their migration from North America to Africa and Asia during the late Pleistocene.

The fastest cheetah ever recorded was clocked at 90 MPH driving a '89 Jaguar with a missing rear bumper. It's unclear who gave that cheetah a driver's license.

Cheetah Story

This one time I was over at my friend Dee's house, having a snack. We heard this scratching at the door and Dee was like, "What? Scratching? Door?"

I totally wasn't scared. I knew it was a cheetah. I've got cheetah sense.

Anyway, we didn't open the door, just to be on the safe side, but you can imagine the kind of hijinks that might have resulted. We had a few snacks, discussed cheetah strategy and tried to draw what the cheetah must've looked like from the sound of his (or her) door scratching technique.
